The Try Ron-A-Thon

"It's not a race, it's a sport"

We're always active on Lake Gage. Back in 2001, we decided to create a mini triathlon of our own on LG during Labor Day Weekend. We named the event after my dad, Ron. We decided that while there would be three activities just like a normal triathlon, all you had to do was "try." Thus the Try Ron-A-Thon was born!
